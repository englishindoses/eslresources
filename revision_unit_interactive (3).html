Meet Tom

Hello! My name is Tom. I am twelve years old and I am from Canada. I have brown hair and blue eyes. I am tall for my age. I have one sister. She is ten years old and her name is Emma.

Today the weather is sunny and warm. I like sunny days because I can play outside. When it's rainy, I stay inside and read books. I have many books in my room.

My family is very nice. My father is a teacher and my mother is a doctor. We have a dog named Max. He is very friendly and loves to play.
